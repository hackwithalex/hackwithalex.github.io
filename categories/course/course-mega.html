what is ethical hacking ?

Ethical hacking is the practice of legally and ethically testing the security of computer systems and networks to identify potential vulnerabilities and prevent unauthorized access. Ethical hackers use the same tools and techniques as malicious hackers but with the intention of improving the security of systems and protecting against potential cyber attacks. It is a critical component of overall cybersecurity and helps organizations stay protected and secure in the ever-evolving digital world.
